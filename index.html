🎯 ATS Resume Tips
What is ATS?
Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) scan and rank resumes before a human reads them. Over 75% of companies use one.

Top Tips to Beat ATS:
✅ Use the exact job title from the posting
✅ Include keywords from the job description naturally
✅ Use standard fonts (Arial, Georgia, Times New Roman)
✅ Avoid special characters, emojis, and icons in the resume body
✅ Keep formatting simple — no text boxes or tables
✅ Save as PDF (not Word) for best compatibility
✅ Use bullet points starting with action verbs
✅ Include a Skills section with exact tool/tech names

Action Verbs that work:
Built, Designed, Led, Managed, Improved, Reduced, Increased, Deployed, Automated, Delivered
